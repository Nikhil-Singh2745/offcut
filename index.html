One sincere interruption

Why make this?

I built this for a hackathon because I wanted to see how far I could push plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript-no libraries, frameworks, or borrowed effects. I could have made another clean, boring webpage. I didn’t want to. This is me treating the browser like a canvas and seeing how much personality I could squeeze out of the basics.

Things I hid in here

  • Click each giant word in the opening headline.
  • Disobey the “Do Not Dilute” stamp.
  • Inspect both printer-registration crosshairs.
  • Question the status light in the header.
  • Interrupt the scrolling services marquee.
  • Ask the wandering Quality Control employee for help.
  • Open every project and inspect what gets stuck to it.
  • Keep pressing the project arrows well past a reasonable point.
  • Paint, clear the evidence, and investigate the fake contact form.
